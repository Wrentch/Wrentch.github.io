Graphic desgin and photography

Little bit about my work and inspiration

I've been into design, photos and generally art for most of my life and have been creating designs since 6th grade. Over the years I've tried and used a wide array of software design tools. From adobe software to mobile apps and paper. I believe that the best work starts with asking the right questions, then combines actionable insights with imagination, and an openness to new ideas creatively driven with a reputation for delivering innovation and simplicity. I've started exploring creative, vibrant and colorful concepts with organic forms and imaginative appeal.

Photography has also been an interest of mine for a long time. Since I got my first moblie phone I've been taking random pictures I thought looked nice. I tried almost everything, from creating random landscapes to photo documentation. Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever… It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything. For me is not looking, it’s feeling. If you can’t feel what you’re looking at, then you’re never going to get others to feel anything when they look at your pictures. It’s about finding something interesting in an ordinary place… I’ve found it has little to do with the things you see and everything to do with the way you see them.
